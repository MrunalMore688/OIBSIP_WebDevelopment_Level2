Tribute to "Sudha Murthy"

The Personification of Simplicity & Philanthropy

Sudha Murty: An engineer, author, and social worker.

About Her Life

Born in 1950 in Shiggaon, Karnataka, Sudha Murty became the first female engineer hired at TATA Engineering and Locomotive Company (TELCO). Beyond her professional success, she is the Chairperson of the Infosys Foundation and a prolific writer in both Kannada and English.

Key Achievements

  • Padma Bhushan (2023): Awarded India's third-highest civilian award for social work.
  • Literary Excellence: Authored best-selling books like 'Wise and Otherwise' and 'The Old Man and His God'.
  • Philanthropy: Spearheaded the building of 2,300 houses in flood-affected areas and thousands of libraries across India.
  • Padma Shri (2006): Recognized for her immense contribution to social work.
